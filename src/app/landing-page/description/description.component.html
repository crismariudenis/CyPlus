CyLiis 19043 is a FIRST Tech Challenge that was founded in 2017, beginning as a team consisting of 15 passionate students, during its 7 years of activity the team gathered contributions from over 100 students from Iași, Romania.

In the 2024-2025 FTC season, the team consists of 30 bright minds pursuing careers in STEM.

Through consistent social media activity, frequent organisation of events to connect with the local community and to spread the FIRST values and with the support provided by Liceul Teoretic de Informatică „Grigore Moisil” Iași we managed to attract more and more students towards the CyLiis Robotics Workshop,
